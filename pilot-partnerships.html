The Year at a Glance

Built around the school calendar, not mine.

Summer

Planning & Alignment

  • Leadership discovery and readiness conversations
  • Review of current AI tools, policies, and priorities
  • Implementation roadmap and initial policy drafting

Outcome: Clear direction before the year begins.

Fall

Launch & Capacity Building

  • Opening PD sessions and teacher AI literacy training

  • Classroom pilot support and leadership advising

Outcome: Teachers start safe, structured implementation.

Winter

Refinement & Expansion

  • Follow-up coaching and targeted workshops
  • Policy refinement based on real classroom use
  • Identification of teacher leaders and internal champions

Outcome: Experimentation becomes consistency.

Spring

Evaluation & Future Planning

  • Implementation review and impact reflection
  • Next-year strategy sessions with leadership
  • Planning for new-staff onboarding and expansion

Outcome: Adoption becomes sustainable practice.
