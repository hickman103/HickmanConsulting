Who I Am

I'm Hunter Hickman — a secondary educator and department leader in Indiana who spends the school day teaching and the rest of it helping schools make AI work.

I've piloted AI-supported writing instruction in my own classroom, run action research on AI tutoring that's now under peer review at an international AI-in-education journal, and trained teachers and leaders across Indiana.

I don't sell a platform. Most districts I work with already pay for tools like Khanmigo or MagicSchool — my job is making that investment actually pay off, and building the AI skills that transfer no matter which tool your district buys next.
